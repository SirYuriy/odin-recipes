Negroni Cocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz gin
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 1 oz Campari

Instructions:

  1. Fill a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Add the gin, sweet vermouth, and Campari to the glass.
  3. Stir until well combined and chilled.
  4. Strain into a chilled glass.
  5. Garnish with an orange slice and enjoy!
