Bramble

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Hendrick's gin
  • 1 oz lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1/2 oz crème de mure (blackberry liqueur)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.
  2. Add the gin, lemon juice, simple syrup, and crème de mure to the shaker.
  3. Shake until well combined and chilled.
  4. Strain into a chilled glass.
  5. Garnish with a blackberry and enjoy!
