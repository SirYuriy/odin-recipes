Side-Car Cocktail

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz cognac
  • 3/4 oz Cointreau
  • 3/4 oz lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Fill a shaker with ice.
  2. Add the cognac, Cointreau, and lemon juice to the shaker.
  3. Shake until well combined and chilled.
  4. Strain into a chilled glass.
  5. Garnish with a lemon twist and enjoy!
